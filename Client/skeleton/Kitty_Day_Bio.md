Everyone can have a calendar app, but can everyone have a Kitty Day Calendar App? With the Kitty Day Calendar, users can create an account, and manage their schedules with unique cat facts for each day, and an assortment of cute kitty profile pictures! In addition, any HTTP status codes that appear will have a cat-coordinated image. Even when the app isn't working right, the user gets their daily dose of kitty cat! Plus, users can assign calendar days with a "Holiday" or "Birthday" Notice, which lets the Kitty Day Calendar App know something special is happening. Using the Animals API, a special cat image will appear on days that are marked as "Holiday" and/or "Birthday". Users can allow permissions to Kitty Day Calendar to receive notifications of their scheduled events, two days prior, and perhaps a day of reminder as well. Notifications can be sent via text, or via email. Users can also add an event to their calendar (create), they can view other National Holidays in the calendar app (Read, I think), they can edit their events (update), or remove an event (delete).
