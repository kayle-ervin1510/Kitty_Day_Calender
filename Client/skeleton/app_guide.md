8-question template - claude_code SPA

1. What problem does the application solve? - This application allows users to keep track of upcoming events, with the added benefit of including a daily cat fact once per day. In addition, users will be able to view their events with images of cats!

2. Who are the users? - Adults, Young Adults, and Teenagers. Children might also use Kitty Day Calender, though they might use it on their parent's phone/profile

3. What are their primary goals? - To have a calender app that keeps track of their events and schedules in a unique way (thus, the kitty images), and brings them a fun cat fact once every day

4. What information do they need? -  They will need a password, email, username, their own name, a preferred name, and to allow app permissions (if they want notifications), and a phone number (optional)

5. What actions can they perform? - They can create events, add events, edit current events, view their current events, choose to view Federal Holidays on their calender, choose to view International Holidays on their calender, choose to add a profile picture (of a cat), and choose a cat photo for their event. 

6. What pages are required? - Login page, Sign Up page, About Kitty Day Calender page, Calender page (in which there is a literal calender), My Events page, Edit Events page, Allow Permissions page.

7. What data must be collected? - Users Time Zone

8. What permissions exist? - Allow App to send notifications to the user's email and/or phone number.
