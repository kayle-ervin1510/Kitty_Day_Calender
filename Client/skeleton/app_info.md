
# App Planning Outline

## Fundamental Questions

### Identifying the App

1. What problem does this app solve? - This app provides users with a fun way to record their schedules. Every day they are given a new Cat Fact, and specific events can include a special kitty image to brighten their day

2. Why would someone use it? - Someone might use the Kitty Day Calender to have a more fun and enjoyable experience logging their schedules, and viewing their listed events

3. What pain point exists today? - Generic calender apps are boring

4. How are users solving this problem? - Users can change the color of their event notification


### Who is the user

1. Who will use this app? - Adults, Young Adults, Teenagers, and possibly Children.

2. There may be additional users. Families might choose to use the Kitty Day Calender App. In which case, users can select: "Make this a Family Account". Then they can add multiple users, all attached to one account. 

3. What information does this user need? - 
	a. Dates of events they want to remember
	b. personal information: email, password, profiles, username, name, and preferred name (optional)
	
### User Goals

1. What actions do people come here to accomplish? - To schedule events on a calender that is unique. This makes scheduling events fun, rather than a chose.

2. Top 3 reasons they login 
	a. View Their schedule and upcoming Events - READ|GET
	b. Creating an event - CREATE|POST
	c. Change or edit an event (updating the time) - UPDATE|PUT

### Information needed
1. What do users view? - User Interface, i.e. the following resources:
	a. profile
	b. Calender
	c. Calender Events
	d. User Events
    e. About Kitty Day
    f. Family Profile

2. What should they never see? - Other user calender data (only the user, or user's family, may see the events they schedule)

3. What do they create, update and delete? - Events that they can schedule

4. What can they not create, update, and delete? - Federal Holidays (these may be hidden, but not deleted), International Holidays(theyse may be hidden, not deleted), Daily Kitty Images, Daily Cat Facts
