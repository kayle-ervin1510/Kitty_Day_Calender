As far as style goes, please have an automatic color scheme similar to Beatrice Potter's Peter Rabbit books (calm pastels). Allow users to change the style and coloring of their calender in their Profile Page. Other styles may include a "Dark Mode", "Light Mode", "Rainbow Mode", "Meow Mix", and a "Mewture Style".

"Dark Mode" keeps pastel coloring for dates, names, and lettering, but the background is always darker (black, dark gray, dark brown, etc.)

"Light Mode" is the default style

"Rainbow Mode" should have a style and theme similar to that of Disney's Super Kitties

"Meow Mixer" should have a style and theme similar to that of the Friskies commercials (rich orange, dark green, tanned brown, and dark brown)

"Meture Style" should have a style like that of the Netflix movie A Whisker Away
